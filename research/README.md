# Research PDF Corpus

This directory preserves the 13 research PDFs reviewed before and during the
WonderLoom build. Each paper was read serially and has a corresponding analytical
report in [`../reports`](../reports). The reports distinguish findings from
author claims, identify limitations and open-source status, and translate the
evidence into product implications without treating short-term engagement as
proof of learning or developmental benefit.
